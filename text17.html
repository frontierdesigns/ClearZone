  • Scope of R&D

AnuLand will investigate a broad range of topics from

  • basic research and applied science through prototyping
  • and small-scale production.
  • Promising products will be tested in pilot studies
  • to generate a solid scientific foundation and proof of efficacy.
  • Results published in the scientific literature; patents procured.
  • Experimental setups will be built to generate real-world
  • data for product development and continued refinement.
  • Small-scale production will give feedback to product
  • development to "debug" products before mass production
