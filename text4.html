  • Opportunity

Establish AnuLand as a natural environmental preserve, a “Clear Zone” community with minimal microwave radiation, chemical contamination and pollution.

Develop AnuLand according to best known practices of governance, finance, health, education, community programs, agriculture, water, energy, waste management. Emphasize family cohesion and personal as well as spiritual growth.

Construct AnuLand using safe building materials according to Building Biology guidelines.

Grow AnuLand as a repository of traditional practices and incubator of beneficial and safe technologies

Aim to make AnuLand self-sufficient through products and services that aid other like-minded communities and the world at large.
