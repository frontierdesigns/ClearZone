  • AnuLand Requirements

Select a suitable tract of acreage to build AnuLand:

    • --large enough to allow for future expansion
    • --provide a sufficient buffer zone to cell phone towers
    • and antennas
    • --close to, but outside of dense urban areas
    • with good potential for land value appreciation
    • --relatively close to an international airport
    • --with sufficient usable terrain to build structures
    • --with natural bodies of water or aquifers, arable soil,
    • and a pleasant climate
